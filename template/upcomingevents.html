Upcoming Events in Kamloops

Kamloops is always bustling with exciting events, from exhilarating outdoor festivals and concerts to art exhibits and sporting events, ensuring there's never a dull moment in this vibrant city.

Wildlights at BC Wildlife Park

Wildlights at the BC Wildlife Park in Kamloops is a magical holiday event that transforms the park into a winter wonderland with over a million twinkling lights. Running from December 13th to January 5th, the event features enchanting light displays, visits from Santa and Mrs. Claus, and performances by beloved characters like Anna and Elsa. There's also a special sensory-friendly evening to ensure everyone can enjoy the festivities. It's a beloved tradition that brings holiday cheer to visitors of all ages.
